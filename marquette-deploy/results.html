Skeptics Guide: Addressing Common Concerns

"Isn't this tone too casual for our brand?"
We've created both casual AND formal versions for A/B testing. The formal version maintains Marquette's professional tone while still incorporating interactive elements. Let the data decide which resonates better with your prospects.
Data: Research shows Gen Z values authenticity over formality, but preferences vary by context. We'll test both approaches and use what works best for Marquette's specific audience.
"How do we know this will work for Marquette specifically?"
That's why we start with a 500-student pilot. We're not asking for a full rollout—just a controlled test with admitted students who already have interest. This minimizes risk while proving concept.
Similar Success: Tulane doubled inquiries, UT Health saw 250% increase in applications, Webster Geneva had 30% enrollment surge—all from interactive content strategies.
"What if the quiz seems unprofessional?"
The quiz maintains academic integrity while being engaging. It highlights real academic programs, actual campus locations, and Marquette's Milwaukee advantages. Think of it as a personalized campus tour, not a BuzzFeed quiz.
Credibility: Questions reference real programs (AIM, 707 Hub), actual locations (Third Ward, Brady Street), and verified statistics (96% job placement).
"Is the ROI actually realistic?"
Even conservative improvements yield massive ROI. If just ONE additional student enrolls from better engagement, that's $200,000+ in lifetime value. The quiz needs just 0.005% improvement to break even.
Math: Average 4-year tuition = $200K. Quiz cost = $0. Break-even = 1 enrollment. Typical result = 5-10 additional enrollments minimum.
"How do we handle the technical implementation?"
Zero IT dependency. The quiz is self-contained HTML that works on any web server. Email templates work in any ESP (Slate, MailChimp, etc.). We provide step-by-step setup guides and training.
Simple Setup: Upload 2 files, add tracking codes, create email campaign. Total setup time: 2-3 hours maximum.
